DIMENSION D6 — Tip Usefulness

Do the tips provide non-obvious, task-specific advice that adds value beyond the steps?
Tips that FAIL this dimension: restate a step already covered ("remember to turn off the
water"), offer generic encouragement ("take your time"), give obvious safety reminders
already in safety_info, or say "call a professional if unsure" without task-specific content.
A passing tip tells the reader something a beginner would not know from the steps alone.
