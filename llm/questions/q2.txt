DIMENSION D2 — Safety Specificity

Does safety_info name the SPECIFIC hazard of this repair AND the SPECIFIC precaution to take?
Generic phrases automatically FAIL: "be careful", "use caution", "stay safe", "be safe",
"exercise caution". A passing safety note names what could go wrong (e.g. "live voltage at
the outlet") and what exact action prevents it (e.g. "flip the breaker and verify with a
non-contact tester before touching wires").
